lingk is designed to be used without requiring any personal information from you. We do not have a login or sign-up system, and therefore we do not collect, store, or process any personal data such as your name, email address, or password.

Log Data & Analytics

We may use basic, anonymous analytics tools to monitor traffic and usage trends for the Service. This information is aggregated and cannot be used to identify any individual user. The data we collect is limited to non-personal information like browser type, country of origin, and pages visited, which helps us improve the service.

Cookies

lingk does not use cookies for tracking or advertising. Any cookies used would be strictly necessary for the technical operation of the website, such as remembering player volume settings.

We encourage you to be aware when you leave our Service and to read the privacy statements of any other site that may collect personally identifiable information.
